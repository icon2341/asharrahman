What is your company going to make? Please describe your product and what it does or will do.

Idea
Why did you pick this idea to work on? Do you have domain expertise in this area? How do you know people need what you're making?
We moved to NYC as transplants and realized that while the city has infinite options, discovery is a broken, multi-app nightmare. If we wanted restaurants with actual seating, we would have to look through the photos. If we wanted to find a spot with real live music, wed have to ask our friends where they had been. We realized that a lot of labour goes into finding things to do. We know people need this because we’ve interviewed 200+ users who are tired of the "5-app planning process". And a similar pattern emerged using "mom test" questions. People are using their notes app to keep a list of places they might go to at some point since searching on demand is time too consuming. We are the right team to automate this labor because we’ve done it before at scale. As founding engineers at Aviary (YC 22), we built scalable RAG systems and multi-agent workflows. We have deep expertise in automated phone orchestration, which is the "last mile" required to turn a search engine into a true agent. To prove it, we’ve already bypassed the Google Places API by building our own proprietary database of all 30,000 NYC restaurants.
Who are your competitors? What do you understand about your business that they don't?
Discovery: Beli and Corner (Manual lists/social graphs; they suffer from "cold start" problems for new users or travelers). Search/Reviews: Google Maps and Perplexity (Rely on static, often outdated indexed data that they buy from places api). Utility: Resy and OpenTable (Only capture a subset of paying venues; invisible to the 60% of venues not on digital platforms). What we understand that they don't: Planning a social outing is a workflow problem, not a search problem. Existing tools provide static data points, but the user still does the "unpaid labor" of vetting a TikTok trend against a Google review and then manually calling to see if it can even handle a party of 8. We understand that real-world data is perishable. By using automated outbound calls to verify real-time availability and leveraging a proprietary dataset of 30,000 NYC venues, we replace a fragmented 5-app planning process with a single agent that actually executes the "last mile" of discovery and booking while also avoiding the "cold start problem" since we don't rely on our users to do the work.
How do or will you make money? How much could you make?
We’ll launch with a free consumer concierge to capture high-signal user behaviors, intent, and actionable insights, then monetize via Pro tier ($12/mo for power users) and sell verified demand to SMBs ($50-$100/mo subscriptions).
